Email Setup Guide

Get Started

Congratulations on your new Track 44 email! You are allowed to do whatever you want with it. This is a private server hosted from QuickPacket, running Ubuntu Server 14.04 and custom iRedMail. It is secure, hosted and maintained by Track 44 staff, and mailboxes are encrypted.

What can you do?

You can use your browser to access the webmail client here, or you can add it to almost any mail application or app, but you will need a few settings. If you would like to apply for an email address, please refer to the registration form.

Common Mail Settings

Protocol/Method: IMAP

"The webmail looks ugly"

For now, there is only one alternate theme included with RoundCube. We are adding more themes in the future. Remember, you can add this email to your existing favorite mail application

For now, you can switch themes by going to the top right settings, then Preferences, User Interface, and choose a "Skin"

"Other people on big email providers aren't getting my emails"

It will take some time and use for the larger email providers like Gmail and Yahoo to "trust" @track44.moe.

The more we use it, the quicker this happens.

Tell your point of contact to check their Spam/Junk folder if they haven't recieved your email.

Requesting Changes

If you want to change your @ there's a few options

- We change your existing username to a new one, you won't be able to receive mail on the old account

- You keep your old one and new one and can log in as both

- We create an "alias" that you can send/recieve as but you login with your old one

- We create your desired @ address and turn your old address into an "alias" you can send/recieve from. You will lose old emails unless you save them as .eml files and import.

All of this can be requested through the registration form or by speaking with a maintainer.

"I forgot my password"

Contact a maintainer on discord or twitter and we can reset your password for you. Make sure you mention someone or your message might get lost
